About WeatherTop

Learn how to unleash the full potential of WeatherTop

Add Station

Add a new weather station by filling out the form at the bottom of your Dashboard view. Open or Delete stations by using the icons on the panel.

Add Reading

Submit a new weather report by using the form inside the station's view.

Weather Summary

The weather summary is displayed after the first reading has been added. Trends for Temperature, Pressure and Wind Speed represented by the up or down arrows get displayed when there is at least three readings in the table. Trends are calculated based on the three latest readings.

Viewing the data

Hover over the values in the table to see the data in different units or a text representing the weather code.

Location of the station

See the area where the WeatherTop station is located by clicking the icon on the panel.
